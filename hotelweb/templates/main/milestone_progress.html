How Milestone Rewards Work

  • Earn Milestone Rewards Every 10 Nights

    Starting at 20 nights, you can claim a reward every 10 nights you stay (20, 30, 40, 50, 60, 70, 80, 90, 100 nights).

  • Choose Your Reward

    At each milestone, choose between 5,000 bonus points or 2 complimentary breakfasts.

  • Annual Reset

    Milestone progress resets each calendar year. Nights count from January 1st to December 31st.

  • Use Your Rewards

    Points are added to your account immediately. Breakfast vouchers can be used on your next booking.
